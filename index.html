About Pavo

Pavo Restaurant represents the pinnacle of fine dining in Abu Dhabi. Nestled in the heart of the city, our establishment combines sophisticated ambiance with award-winning chef-driven cuisine. Each dish is meticulously crafted using the finest local and international ingredients.

Our culinary philosophy celebrates innovation while honoring time-honored traditions. Led by our award-winning chef, our kitchen team brings decades of combined experience from Michelin-starred establishments around the world.

At Pavo, we believe dining is not just about food—it's about creating unforgettable moments. Our warm and professional staff ensures every guest experiences impeccable service in an elegant setting designed for comfort and conversation.

Our Values: Quality ingredients, culinary excellence, warm hospitality, and sustainable practices.
